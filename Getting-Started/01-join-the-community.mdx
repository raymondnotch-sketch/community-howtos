# Join Codetopia Community

Welcome! We are glad you are here.

Codetopia Community is a place for builders, learners, and creators
figuring it out together. We learn in the open, build real things, and
help each other level up. Everyone here started as a beginner, and
nobody is expected to know everything.

Want the fuller story before you join? Read our
[about page](https://community.codetopia.org/about) and our
[code of conduct](https://community.codetopia.org/code-of-conduct).

Joining happens in two parts:

1. **Create your portal account**, which gives you your identity in the
   community (this page covers it)
2. **Join our Discord server**, which is where the community talks every
   day (the next guide covers it)

Let's start with the first one.

---

## What is the portal?

The portal is the community's home base. Every member has an account
there. It is your identity in the community, and it is where the real
work happens: you join teams, submit your work for review, and keep
track of everything you are involved in.

Think of it like this: your portal account is your membership card. You
create it once, and it opens every door in the community, including
access to our Discord server.

---

## Before you start

You will need two things:

- A working **email address** that you can open and read right now. You
  will receive a verification email during signup, so make sure you can
  get to your inbox.
- Your **correct personal information**. Use your real name and real
  details. Members who sign up with information that does not belong to
  them will have their accounts removed or banned without notice.

---

## Create your account

1. Open your browser and go to
   [portal.community.codetopia.org](https://portal.community.codetopia.org)
2. Follow the steps on the screen to create your account. Read everything
   carefully and take your time. There is no rush.
3. The portal will walk you through a short onboarding. A few screens
   that introduce you to the community and ask for the details we need.
   Fill everything in correctly and completely.
4. During onboarding you will be asked to agree to our **code of
   conduct**. Read it properly. It is short, and it is what keeps this
   community a good place to be. Agreeing to it is part of being a
   member.

**You will know you are done** when you can log in to the portal and see
your own account.

---

## What's next?

Your portal account is ready. Now it is time to join the community on
Discord, where all the conversations, learning, and collaboration happen.
